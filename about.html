About Me

About the Author

Drawing comics has always been Jonathan Ang's passions and hobbies. During school times, he would draw comics for his classmates to see. Jonathan's comics are mostly comedy adventure stories that sometimes don't make any sense. Overtime, Jonathan started to gain more passion in storytelling in his comics, thus he started branching out to other genres, though most of the time his comics remain funny to read and inspirational for any people who needs words of support.




History of Jonathan's Comics

Brief History of comic-drawing hobby.



The Beginning of the Instagram Era (2019-)

Before 2019, Jonathan thought it was impossible to draw comics on Instagram. However, after "The Christmas Disaster" comic release on Instagram, Jonathan then began to draw comics on Instagram frequently as well.

The Beginning of the Facebook Era (2018-)

Moving to college, Jonathan started to write comics in Facebook more. He started writing stories for Facebook friends to see, and since then, has never stopped drawing comics in Facebook.

Pinang A's Era (2015-2017)

Jonathan started drawing inspirational and Christian comics to send to his friends on chats. He also started drawing motivational comics on Facebook. DUe to his interest in a video game (Five Nights at Freddy's), he started drawing FNAF comics for his friends in school until he graduated from middle school.

First Graphic Novel (2014)

Jonathan made a handdrawn colored graphic novel about surviving a mega tsunami. It took eleven months to complete the comic. This was the first big project ever done.

Childhood Hero (2013)

Jonathan began drawing a lot about his favorite cartoon character, Ben 10. Sadly those comics were cleared off by his mother. Nothing was recovered. :(

Primary School Era (2009-2012)

Started out in primary school drawing comics about his own bizzare adventures with his friends. The comics were smuggled on his classmates' notebooks for them to see.
